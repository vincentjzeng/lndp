1. How to update the footer ad
a. login to http://www.alimama.com/ with vincentellan (Taobal account) and make to ad
b. Update the ad.properties to the target ad
c. restart lndp to make the ad taking effect.

2. How to deploy/redeploy lndp
a. Backup all the config files in the common classes folder "common\classes" of Tomcat.
b. Backup the project folder "lndp\userfiles"
c. Backup the project "common"
d. Backup DB.
e. Redeply DB, common and lndp projects.
